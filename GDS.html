Upcoming Talk

May 22 at 1:30pm (Beijing time)

Xun Yu (Tianjin University)

Title: Automorphism groups of smooth hypersurfaces

Abstract: In this talk, I will discuss some recent results about classifying automorphism groups of smooth hypersurfaces in the projective space. This talk is based on joint works with Keiji Oguiso, Li Wei, Song Yang, and Zigang Zhu.
