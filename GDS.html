Upcoming Talk

December 6 at 1:30pm (Beijing time)

Jia Jia (Tsinghua University)

Title: Wild automorphisms of compact complex spaces

Abstract: An automorphism of a compact complex space is called wild if there is no non-trivial proper invariant analytic subset. In this talk, I will show that a compact complex surface admitting a wild automorphism is either a complex torus or an Inoue surface of certain type, and this wild automorphism has zero entropy. This is based on a joint work with Long Wang.
