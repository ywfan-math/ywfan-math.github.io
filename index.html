Welcome!

I am a Morrey Visiting Assistant Professor of Mathematics at UC Berkeley.

My research interest lies broadly in algebraic geometry, dynamical systems, and symplectic geometry. I am particularly interested in:
  • Derived categories and birational geometry
  • Mirror symmetry between algebraic geometry and symplectic geometry
  • Bridgeland stability conditions
  • Categorical dynamical systems
