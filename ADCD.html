Online Seminar on Algebraic and Complex Dynamics

Description

Welcome to the Online Seminar on Algebraic and Complex Dynamics.

This is a new seminar, centered loosely around algebraic dynamics, complex dynamics, and related topics. We meet weekly for a 90-minute talk, with the first 30 minutes aiming towards non-experts and graduate students. After the talk we will have 30 minutes of informal discussion.

The seminar usually takes place on Tuesdays, either at 9am in Beijing/Taipei (= UTC 1am) or at 3pm in Beijing/Taipei (= UTC 7am), depending on where the speakers are located. The talks will be hosted on Zoom. All are welcome to tune in.

Upcoming Talk

December 20 at 3pm in Beijing/Taipei

Keiji Oguiso (University of Tokyo)

Title: Calabi-Yau threefolds with c_2-contractions-revisited

Abstract: The second Chern class $c_2$ plays a special role in studying Calabi-Yau threefolds. Among other things, we discuss about KSC (Kawaguchi-Silverman's Conjecture) and the finiteness problem of real forms of Calabi-Yau threefolds with $c_2$-contractions. Though nothing appears in the title and main statements, our arguments use dynamical degrees due to Dinh-Sibony and current work on slow dynamics due to Dinh-Lin-O-Zhang in essential ways.
