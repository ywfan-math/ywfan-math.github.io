Online Seminar in Algebraic and Complex Dynamics

Description

Welcome to the Online Seminar in Algebraic and Complex Dynamics.

This is a new seminar, centered loosely around algebraic dynamics, complex dynamics, and related topics. We meet weekly for a 90 minutes talk, with the first 30 minutes aiming towards non-experts and graduate students. After the talk we will have 30 minutes of informal discussion.

The seminar usually takes place on Tuesdays at 3pm (China) / 4pm (Japan, Korea) / 9am (CET), unless otherwise adjusted for the speakers. The talks will be hosted on Zoom. All are welcome to tune in.
