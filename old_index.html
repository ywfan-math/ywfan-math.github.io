Welcome!

I am an assistant professor at Yau Mathematical Sciences Center of Tsinghua University in Beijing.

My research interest lies broadly in algebraic geometry, dynamical systems, symplectic geometry, and mathematical physics. I am particularly interested in:
  • Derived categories and birational geometry
  • Mirror symmetry between algebraic geometry and symplectic geometry
  • Bridgeland stability conditions
  • Categorical dynamical systems
