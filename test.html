YU-WEI FAN

I'm David,


I am an assistant professor at the Yau Mathematical Sciences Center at Tsinghua University in Beijing. I was previously at UC Berkeley as a Morrey visiting assistant professor, and did my PhD with Professor Shing-Tung Yau at Harvard.


My research interest lies broadly in algebraic geometry, dynamical systems, symplectic geometry, and mathematical physics. I am particularly interested in derived categories, mirror symmetry, stability conditions, and categorical dynamical systems.
